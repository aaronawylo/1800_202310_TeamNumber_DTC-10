About us

Whether you're a student with no money or a homeowner looking to insure their living space, Active Reactive will help you Actively React to whatever comes your way.

Frequently Asked Questions

Active Reactive grants its users access to all of their emergency needs in one location. If you have any questions about Health, Supplies or Finances caused by various weather emergencies, we have you covered!

During a weather crises, panicing is the last thing you want to do. It is only during crises that we start thinking about financial aid, health survices, where to find food and suplies, and even mental health support in the following days.

Registering with us only requires an email and password, and will allow you to save any articles that you find useful to your profile so that you can always comeback to read them at a later date, or if an emergency occurs.

Our weather articles consist of information reguarding:

  • Heat
  • Cold
  • Flood
  • Fire
  • Thunderstorm
  • Wind
  • EarthQuake
All of these articles inform our users of critical information and aid that might be needed in such events.

We have a variety of benefit articles including:

  • Health - Illnesses, disease, hostpitals, warning signs
  • Supplies - Food supplies, emergencty kits, non-profit organizations
  • Finances - Financial aid, guides and more!
